About Me

I'm the co-Founder & CEO of Ganddee - a climate-tech bringing sustainable consumption to the masses.

I'm CS educated, specialized in networking & security. I've spent many years in startups, from engineering to exec positions. Before starting Ganddee I was Head of R&D at Clearmatics, a London-based FinTech where I worked on zero-knowledge proof systems, designed and implemented decentralized trading protocols, crypto-derivatives and more.

I'm also a startup advisor and an engineering consultant helping FinTechs design decentralized/cryptographic protocols, use zero-knowledge proofs and/or blockchain to meet their needs. I've worked with companies like Osmosis, Flashbots and QED-it.
